About

I am a Nashville-based software developer with a concentration in front-end design. My background is quite varied, with experience in psychology, music-based creative work, and client-facing service. My diverse experience has shaped me into a strategic thinker who understands exactly what a client is looking for, and how to implement it effectively.

I enjoy work that encourages me to think outside of the box to solve problems and thrive in environments that foster creativity, authenticity, and inclusivity. I look forward to creating useful and aesthetically pleasing applications that make a difference in the lives of others, whether that be by making their day-to-day life a bit easier or by simply bringing a smile to their face.
